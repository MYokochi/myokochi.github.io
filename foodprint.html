Food Print

Created for Mossy Earth

An infographic on the environmental impact of diet and lifestyle.

Mossy Earth is a team dedicated to restoring Nature and fighting climate change. Their projects range from restoring wild ecosystems, planting native trees and aiding in the conservation of endangered species. In addition to the ground work they also share educational content through a rewilding blog, a podcast, and living guides that give insight into the impact of different lifestyles on the environment.

I was asked to develop this infographic as part of the lifestyle guides on their website as well as to be shared on social media. It was done in collaboration with one of Mossy Earth’s conservation biologists. He explained how they were looking for a way to communicate the findings of different studies, on the impact of diet and lifestyle on the environment, in a captivating visual way.

The Problem

How can we share this data on the impact of diet and lifestyle on the environment in a captivating visual way?

The Goal

The goal of this project was to bridge the gap between the scientific papers that inform Mossy Earth’s efforts and the public. To share them in a tangible way, spread awareness on personal carbon footprint and help people make more informed decisions on their diets. Ultimately to help in the understanding of the lifestyle guides and, through social media, to get people interested in their mission and direct them to the website.
